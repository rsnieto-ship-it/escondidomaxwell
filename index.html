Welcome to

Your Hidden Escape

Nestled on 10 private acres in Maxwell, Texas, Quinta Escondida offers the perfect escape from the everyday. Wake up to pastoral views of grazing cattle, spend your days in peaceful solitude, and end each evening under a canopy of stars in our dark sky paradise.

Whether you're seeking a quiet weekend getaway, a family gathering, or simply a place to disconnect and recharge, our secluded retreat welcomes you with warm hospitality and rustic Texas charm.
